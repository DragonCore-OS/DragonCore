# Huaxia Persona Registry | 华夏人物库

The 19 seats are the frozen core. DragonCore maintains an **extensible Huaxia Persona Registry** for secondary institutions, codename systems, and reserve governance roles.

This registry currently defines **30+ mythic and historical figures** from Chinese civilization.

---

## Usage

### Secondary Institution Selection

Use registry figures to staff secondary offices:

```
韩非司 — Governance & Permissions
张衡台 — Monitoring & Dashboards
扁鹊局 — System Diagnosis
太乙台 — Crisis Repair
狄仁杰司 — Anomaly Investigation
墨子局 — Cost Optimization Engineering
后羿组 — Critical Bottleneck Elimination
共工台 — Destructive Testing
```

### Project Codenames

Name special initiatives using registry figures:

```
李白计划 — Brand Content System
韩非计划 — Permission Governance & Approval Chain
孙武计划 — Competitive Strategy & Market Attack/Defense
墨子计划 — Cost Compression Engineering
张衡计划 — System Observation Platform
扁鹊计划 — Diagnosis & Early Warning System
```

### Selection Principles

1. **Function first, persona second**: Have organizational needs first, then match personas
2. **Conflict is a feature**: Choose naturally conflicting personas to create organizational tension
3. **Power drives must be explicit**: Every persona needs clear power_drive and red_lines
4. **Tiered hierarchy**: core_governance > supervisory > warband > special_office
